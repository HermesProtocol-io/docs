Welcome to the documentation of Hermes Protocol.

Hermes Protocol is a communications platform that allows users to receive instant alerts about on-chain events, such as wallet transactions (whale monitoring, for ex.), NFT purchases/bids/lists/etc., price alerts, governance polls, validator profile changes (commission %, name, etc.), & more.

:::info

**Supported Blockchains**: Terra, Terra Classic. _(up-to-date list can be found [here](https://github.com/HermesProtocol-io/assets/tree/main/static/blockchains))_

:::

## üö© Published Notifications

### üîî Price Alerts

Through the **Price Alert** notification, be alerted about price changes of cryptocurrencies you're looking to buy or sell.

### üè¶ Governance Poll

With the **Governance Poll** notification, subscribe to governance polls created on the supported blockchains and their dApps as well.

### üßæ NFT Monitor

The **NFT Monitor** notification has multiple options so people can subscribe to sales/mints of entire NFT collections, or other actions like creating auctions, bids, listings, etc., or perhaps specify just a few NFT's they'd like to _snipe_, instead of entire collections.

### üíº Wallet Watcher

The **Wallet Watcher** notification allow users to follow all transactions made to or from one or multiple wallets.
