"Broadcast Communications" are the transmission of simultaneous messages or notifications to a wide audience (aggregated by Contact Lists). Hermes Protocol empowers businesses to seamlessly manage and disseminate broadcast communications within the web3 landscape, safeguarding user privacy and optimizing deliverability.
