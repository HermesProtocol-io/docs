To obtain Subscribers via Telegram, it's essential to have the Hermes Protocol bot added to your community. Start by opening a chat with our bot through [this link](https://t.me/HermesProtocolBot), and then open its Telegram Profile and choose "Add to Group".

![create list](/img/broadcast/telegram-auth.png)

:::info
You can only invite the bot to a Group where you are an Admin.
