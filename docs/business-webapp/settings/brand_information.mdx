The Brand Information is the publicly shown information about your Organization. It'll be present in all your Communications so that users understand who is sending them messages and why they received them.

![brand_information](/img/settings/brand_main.png)

:::info
The Brand Logo has the following format limitations:
- jpeg
- png
- webp
- gif

Other formats are not supported on our system as some social platforms do not support them yet.
:::
