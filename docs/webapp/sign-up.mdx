1- Go to the sign up page [here](https://business.hermesprotocol.io/signup)

![Sign up screen](/img/signup/mainscreen.png)

2- Click in Connect Wallet button

3- Read and accept the [terms of use](https://hermesprotocol.io/terms) and [privacy policy](https://hermesprotocol.io/privacy_policy) by checking the box

4- After confirming the acceptance of terms you will be able to choose the wallet that you want to connet:
![choose wallet pop-up](/img/signup/choose_wallet.png)

5- If you do not have a certain wallet extension, the options to install will show up. Install one of our compatible providers and import your wallet to proceed.

6- Click in "Verify Wallet" button to complete the sign up process. This step is necessary to prove your ownership of the wallet. <ins>This action does not have costs.</ins> 

7 - You can setup the [2F Authentication](2FA) or skip the step by clicking in the Sign up button.
