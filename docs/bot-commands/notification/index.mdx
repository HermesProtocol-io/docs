
The Hermes Protocol bot offers a versatile and user-friendly way to set up and manage your customized notifications for a variety of events, such as NFT monitor, governance changes, price fluctuations, and more. To get started, simply type "/" in any channel where the bot is present, and you'll be presented with a list of available notification. Follow the guided steps to configure your preferred alerts, ensuring you stay informed and connected to the blockchain activity that matters most to you.
